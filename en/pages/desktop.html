🚀 Installation in 3 steps

1
Download the installer – Click "Download Installer" above and save the file.
2
Run the setup – Double-click SafeNet.Security.Setup.1.0.0.exe. If Windows SmartScreen appears: click «More info» → «Run anyway».
3
Open the app – After installation, you'll find SafeNet Security on your desktop and in the Start Menu.
